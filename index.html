Sometimes good things happen

and sometimes things are bad

So I'm bouncy and happy

And sometimes a bit sad

It's a beautiful cycle

A simple little fact

Feelings come from Reason

It's cause and effect.

Reason guides Emotion

They go hand in hand

They dance till they get tired

and go to sleep in the end.

But sometimes...

Happiness comes alone

And throws a party

Without Reason.

It brings joy and warmth,

wanting more and more

then the party slowly

goes out of control.

Mind rushing!

Racing thoughts!

Too much light!

Too much noise!

Overwhelmed by it all,

It is no longer fun.

I wish it was over

But the worst is yet to come.

Lump in my throat,

I'm scared and teary.

Please please please,

Don't let anyone see me!

I hide in my room

Shivering and crying

Dark and heavy thoughts

Circling around me

I feel so weak and helpless

And hate myself for it.

There a beautiful world out there,

I just don't deserve to live in it.

What did I do wrong?

Why do I feel this way?

If I find the reason

this pain might go away!

Hours later I'm still there,

Exhausted and broken.

Chest pain and self hate...

I could just slit...

My wrist...

Wide open.

I'll cry myself to sleep

I can do this.

I've been practising it

since I was six.

I crawl under the duvet,

I wish that I was dead,

Then I hear a faint purr

From the corner of the bed

Someone still needs me,

my sweet little cat

I can't end my life now

Can't give up just yet.

I wake up the next day

It's quiet and calm

And I'm so grateful

That I did no self harm

The pain is still there

And I don't know why,

I'm scared to tell anyone

But ashamed to lie

So, for a few days

I just stay at home

I'll see my friends when I'm happy

but I get through this on my own.

Things will be ok again

I just need a little time

I know you are on my side

So don't worry, I'm fine.

Life is back to normal now

I work, I play, I sleep

No one sees my scars

Hiding underneath

This can only destroy

One person - that's me

So I will never get married

Or start a family

I don't have a future

Just a short term plan

To love and feel loved

Before this happens... again.
